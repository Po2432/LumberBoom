📱 How to Install on iPhone (Offline Play)

  1. Tap the Play in Browser button above.
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow up) at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Launch from your home screen to play without internet!
